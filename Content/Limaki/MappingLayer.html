Things, Links and Graphs

The mapping layer connects the model with the view. This is realized with a GraphPair.
A GraphPair is a facade on two Graphs of different types, Graph<Widget> and Graph<Thing>. To the presentation layer it apperears as a Graph<Widget>, but every operation is performed on the Graph<Thing> too. So a visual abstraction of the model can be done by creating a GraphPair<Widget, Thing>.
